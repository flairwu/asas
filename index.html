Guatemala

Officially the Republic of Guatemala, graces Central America with its captivating tourist attractions like the iconic Mayan ruins and the charming town of Antigua. Home to roughly 9 million Guatemalans, it has the largest economy in Central America as one of the main export countries.

The Reality

Guatemala’s Poverty & Inequality

Yet, beyond the tourist photos lies a tale of persistent poverty and inequality.
Approximately 4.6 million Guatemalans grapple for daily sustenance,
Roughly 9 million citizens live on less than $2.15 per day, placing them below the poverty line.
Behind these numbers lie stories of children missing out on education and prospects, women denied opportunities, ailing individuals facing inadequate healthcare, and neglected seniors. Guatemalans paints the often-unseen reality of Guatemala with their blood and tears.

Guatemala's Initiative For Tomorrow

We are a none profit organization based in Canada that's dedicated to making a difference for the less fortunate in Guatemala. The unjust they experienced is already history, so we focus on what we can today in order to gift them a better tomorrow! Working with our partner the Association for EDUSI who are based in Guatemala, we have 4 main programs : Gift to Children, Gift to the unwell, Gift to women and Gift to elder. They each target the most vulnerable groups in Guatemala. There is so much we can do with the help of your kindness, learn about our programs and how you can help us make a difference !

The Four Program

GIFT
To The
Children

• Gift to the Children

Learn More

GIFT to the Children Program aims to provide children of school age with the proper education they need. Currently the program runs from K to high school with 4 main sectors: providing necessary materials, sponsoring children in extrema poverty to attend existing local institutions, after school program to reinforce what they learned at school and finally providing health nutrition to keep their body healthy so their mind can make wonders.

GIFT
to the
Women

• Gift to the Women

Learn More

GIFT to the Women programs aims to address the genders inequality in Guatemala by empowering and educating women. The areas we cover are: Human Rights and Women’s rights, Literacy, Crafts and Self-sufficiency, Leaderships and start ups. Each to help the women of Guatemala to be strong independent and safe. Also we host a array of events and marches to spread awareness on the issue.

Learn More

GIFT
to the
Elder

• Gift to the Elder

Learn More

GIFT to the Elder programs aims to improve the living conditions of senior adults in vulnerable situations. Currently we provide nutritious meals adequate to their needs, fun physical exercises to matin and improve their health. Our Medical clinic provides care and medical supplies, and lastly we host a array of workshops focused on developing and or maintain the cognitive and social functions. Our goal to to keep these elders as active and valuable members of society.

• Gift to the Unwell

Learn More

GIFT to the Unwell Program aims to provide medical services to improve the short to medium term well being of the population. Our Ambulatory Free Clinics act as the emergency room and provide more short term and argent care, providing both medical consultation as well as medications free of charge. Our Primary Health care focus more on the medium term, educating on topic regarding health and wellbeing philocaly and emotionally.
